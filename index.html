ABOUT US

From unforgettable pancakes to Famous chicken & waffles, to mouth-watering burgers to wings that make Buffalo jealous… Our family made it’s name synonymous with good eats in the Southwest, Ohio area beginning with the Golden Nugget Pancake House carrying on the tradition with Doubleday’s Grill & Tavern. Now enjoy a fresh take on storied family dishes along with a few items you may not find anywhere else with The Famous Restaurant.

We invite you to stop in to enjoy a comfortable, relaxed atmosphere centered around good food and great people. Experience a new take on some of your favorites or be bold and try something new on our ever changing Famous menu
